SideLine is a power side-channel attack vector based on delay-line components widely implemented in modern CPUs.

There is a strong and reliable relationship between the delay-line state and your CPU activity.

SideLine can be maliciously used to monitor your CPU power activity without your consent. Secured enclosures, access or tamper resistance do not mitigate Sideline. It can be launched remotely even it is a hardware attack

A CPU infected by a SideLine-based malware is suceptible to the theft of personal and private data such as cryptographic secrets.
